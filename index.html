Meet the Candidates

Henry Reyes - Presidential Candidate

Henry Brayden Reyes was born on July 25, 1972 in Grand Rapids, Michigan to Nancy Smith and Jonathan Reyes, an major real estate developer. Henry graduated from Calvin University with a 3.4 GPA, and took the reins from his father, Jonathan, and turned his real estate empire into an commercial success. In 2024, Reyes decided he was going to run for the Republican nomination for President, in which he won. Reyes is known for his negotiation skills from the real estate industry and his quick wit. He currently stands between Barack Obama, where he will hopefully prevail.

Calvin Edwards - Vice Presidential Candidate

Calvin Edwards is a Representative from South Carolina's 5th congressional district. He graduated from Harvard Business School, and started his own Business back in the year 2008, and since then he has been growing it. He got famous from his business's and his congressional career, many famous like his ideas that he as planned, including his running mate Henry Reyes, that is one of the reasons that he chose Calvin as his running mate. Calvin is an experienced politician, dreaming since he was a little boy to help his country, he is very active in sports, water polo, football, swimming and basketball! He likes hanging out with his family & friends, going to all kinds of places and helping his country.
